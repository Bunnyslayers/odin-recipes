Steak

Description

Today we will learn to cook a steak with a crispy crust and a nice pink interior.

Ingredients

  • steak
  • salt and pepper
  • butter
  • avacado oil
  • cast iron pan

Steps

  1. Rest steak to room temparture
  2. Heat avocado oil in cast iron pan
  3. Season steak with salt and pepper
  4. Place steak in pan and sear for 2 minutes on both sides
  5. Turn heat to low and add butter
  6. Baste steak with butter until an internal temp of 125 is reached
  7. Remove steak and rest for 10 minutes
