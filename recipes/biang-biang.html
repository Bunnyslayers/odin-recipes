Biang Biang

Description

Biang Biang noodles are a belt noodle native to china. It consists of wide and thick noodles that are first covered in spices and aromatics, etc:paprika, chili flakes, curry powder, and then having very hot oil poured over.

Ingredients

  • Belt Noodles
  • Paprika
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Place belt noodles in boiling water. Contrary to common practice in italian cuisine there is no need to salt the water. Boil until tender but still chewy.
  2. Heat a 1/4 cup of oil in a pot until just below smoke point.
  3. While noodles are boiling combine 2 tbsp Soy Sauce with 1 tbsp of Rice Vinegar, chop 3 cloves of garlic, and chop a 1in by 1in in knob of ginger.
  4. Drain cooked noodles and place desired amount into bowl. Pour sauce overnoodles, and place desired amount of garlic, ginger, and paprika on top of noodles.
  5. Now be very careful with this step. Slowly pour, or ladle, oil over the noodles, making sure to soak all of the spices and auromatics.
  6. Mix well and enjoy, hopefully, with chopsticks.
